Book The Truck

Del Popolo is available for private events within fifty miles of Orlando or Miami. We’ll drive the truck to your location, bake our pizza on-site and serve it buffet-style or as a passed appetizer fresh out of the oven. While the menu is typically comprised of three or four different pizzas, we can also provide salad and non-alcoholic beverages for an additional reasonable fee. Currently, the pizzeria's small plates are not available as part of our catering menu.

Logistically, the truck is big - 30 feet long by 8 feet wide - so we need about thirty-five feet of relatively flat parking with fifteen feet of overhead clearance. It’s also important that access roads to the event venue are suitable for a large truck.

Pricing is based on a variety of factors, so please provide us with the following information and we’ll get back to you with our availability and a price quote. Typically, events makes the most sense when they consist of 50 or more people, and our minimum in Orlando is $1,875 exclusive of tax.
